About

Hi! I’m a sound designer and musician, my name is Andrew Widdis. I’ve had experience as an in-house Sound Designer located in Southampton. I've worked on collaborative projects ranging from video games to short film.

I have a fondness for hardware samplers. I use samplers to process all my sounds (whether SFX, foley, or music) and through the years have developed a good ear for sound. Feel free to contact me here.
